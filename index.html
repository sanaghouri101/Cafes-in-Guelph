Hi! My name is Sana Ghouri :)
I’m a Marketing Management Co-op student in my final year at the University of Guelph. This semester, I explored local cafés around Guelph and curated my top four favourites, taking into account atmosphere, price, and flavour. As someone who enjoys both studying and relaxing in café spaces, I wanted to highlight spots that offer a great overall experience.

Below are featured spots:

  • Café Galata – An authentic Turkish café and restaurant serving traditional Turkish Coffee along with freshly baked goods.
  • Centurion Coffee – A modern café offering carefully brewed coffee and a relaxed vibe.
  • Sol Café – A bright, welcoming spot with a variety of unique drinks and light bites.
  • Cavan – A café with a strong mission of ethical coffee sourcing.

I hope you enjoy my café reviews and find them helpful!
